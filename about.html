- About -

The past few decades have introduced some spectacular female artists into the music world. Thanks to modern marketing and commercialism, only certain names are noticed on an international scale. Let's face it, commercial success is not a measure of an artists talent though, its the money-machine behind them that determines visibility.

This site is focused on a particular niche of global music. The female artists of the Nordic region. Please enjoy the gallery of performers we have compiled so far.

Do you know of an Artist we should feature? Please let us know.. After all, it's music to our ears!
